Discussion Question

1) What's your modification? Any thoughts (why)? Steps?

For the 1st editing image: I added some Exposure (Brightness (+20), Contrast (+50), Highlight (+20) and Shadow (-30) to improve the lighting quality of the image. Since I edited the image online (befunky.com), the steps are quite easy, all you need to do is select the free options available and adjust the bar to see the differences in the image.

For the 2nd editing image: I used Adobe Photoshop to transform the original image into sketched/drawing image. I Applied the sRGB Curved to change the colours into black and white, then applied Hue and Saturation to vivid the lining details of the image, then use Color Dodge and Blur to enhance it.

2) What are the differences you notice when you created/drew the new image(s)?

For the 1st editing image: The quality after I touched the image is improving alot, especially with the lighting in th background. With that being said, all the changes and improvement has been applied and therefore, increase the picture size for a bit.

For the 2nd editing image: The quality after Io transform from colours image into sketched/drawing image has been reduced. Since there is no colours, the image size has been reduced for about 25%, and also, when zoom in to the image this image tends to have lower quality compared to the first 2.

3) Is there any special consideration when you created/drew the new image(s)? What were the steps?

All I want to do when I create the new image is to make the look as much better or similar to the original one, therefore, I have to use some image editing app to help me with that.
